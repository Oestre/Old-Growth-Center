Standing orders

  • 3-second rule — after any opponent blunder, and before any check or capture: hands off, three seconds, ask "what did that move stop defending?" (27.Nh4?? · 28...Ne2+??)
  • Vienna sidelines — 2...Bc5 3.Qg4 Qf6 → only 4.Qg3. The 3...Nxe4 trick → only 4.Qh5.
  • Anti-Alapin — vs 3.c3: 3...d5 always, 4...Bg4!, and vs Qa4+ block with 5...Qd7. Never 3...Nc6.
  • Games fixes — vs French: commit to 3.e5 Advance. vs Sicilian as White: adopt the Alapin. vs 1.d4: pick one defense and stay.
